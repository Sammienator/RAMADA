About Me

WHO IS CLOUD GALAXY.

Cloud galaxy is a kenyan Tanzanian RNB musician born and raised partly in both countries Tanzania and Kenya. Cloud Galaxy is a musical prodigy from East Africa. The Tanzanian born Artist's career quickly gained momentum with the release of “#Tabasamu,” a song off his critically acclaimed first album, to be released in 2023.

Why CLOUD GALAXY.

Cloud galaxy is a talented and well disciplined musician, singer, songwriter,and al round Artist. Above all God fearing and equally determined in every aspect of his music and life. Cloud Galaxy is One of the new voices energizing the BONGO Flava music scene, His words and melodies are deeply ingrained in a charming consciousness that permeates his music. A real talent and energetic performer.
